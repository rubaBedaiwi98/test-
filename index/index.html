UNI-Guider

The student guide for unified electronic admission in public and private universities in Jeddah (King Abdulaziz University, Jeddah University, Effat University, Dar Al-Hekma University) as this guide provides you with all the instructions and procedures necessary to apply for the unified electronic admission portal.

Good to Know

Joining request

The student fills out the application form by himself and she/he is responsible for the correctness of the information recorded.

Ranking of majors

The good selection and arrangement of majors is one of the basic tasks that the student must give them all care and attention.

Banning enrolled students

The regulations of universities prevent the student from applying to the university in which he is enrolled.
